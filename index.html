Taehyung, Lisa, Bogum slay airport fashion as they leave for Celine show in Paris

On June 27, a trio of top Korean stars, BTS’s V, BLACKPINK’s Lisa, and actor Park Bo Gum arrived at Seoul Gimpo airport to catch the flight to Paris. They will reportedly head to Paris via the same private plane and attend CELINE Men’s Summer Fashion Show 2023.

CELINE Men’s Summer Fashion Show 2023 will take place at 4 pm KST on June 26. Netizens and fans are now looking forward to the interactions between V, Lisa, and Park Bo Gum. If the three take pictures together at the show, they promise to take social media by storm.

Lisa raises expectations for her appearance at CELINE’s upcoming fashion show as the brand’s first and only global ambassador.
V has been a sought-after face by several luxury designer brands, from Louis Vuitton, Gucci, to CELINE. He is expected to be the first BTS member to gain a foothold in the fashion industry, with his overflowing charisma and popularity.
Having a handsome face and exuding elegance, Park Bo Gum left a good impression when he attended Louis Vuitton Men’s Fall-Winter 2022 fashion show that took place not long ago. His upcoming appearance at CELINE’s show in Paris promises to take his position in the fashion world to a higher level.
